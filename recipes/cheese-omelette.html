Cheese Omelette

A quick, simple, yet filling breakfast, perfect for starting your day.

It only needs 10 minutes to make, making 1 serving in the process.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp of milk
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • Â¼ cup of shredded emmentaler cheese (can be any cheese)
  • salt
  • freshly grounded white pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Pour the milk and season with salt & white pepper.
  3. Whisk the mixture for ~2-3 minutes to make it foamy.
  4. Pour the egg mixture and swirl the skillet to fully cover it w/ the mixture.
  5. Cook for ~1 minute to set it.
  6. Lift the edges w/ a skillet so the uncooked egg can flow to the bottom.
  7. Repeat until no visible liquid remains.
  8. Carefully flip the omelette and continue to cook it for ~30 seconds - 1 minute.
  9. Sprinkle the cheese in the middle and fold in half.
  10. Cook until cheese is melted (~20 seconds)
  11. Slide the omelette onto the plate, add your favorite garnish, and enjoy.
