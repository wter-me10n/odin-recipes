Oreo Cheesecake

A rich, delicious and chocolatey delight for all cheesecake lovers to savour.

It requires at least 4 hours to make, creating 12 servings in the process.

Ingredients

  • 24 oreos divided (16 for crust, 8 for filling)
  • 3 tbsp of melted butter
  • 3 (8 oz) packages of cream cheese
  • ¾ cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 3 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Put 16 oreos into a resealable bag. Remove excess air then seal.
  3. Finely crush the oreos via using a rolling pin.
  4. Fill the bowl with the oreos and the melted butter, and mix well.
  5. Press the mixture firmly onto the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  6. Beat the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla in a bowl and use an electric mixer on medium speed until it's well-blended.
  7. Beat eggs one at a time until blended.
  8. Chop 8 cookies, gently stir half of them into the cheese batter.
  9. Pour the crust, and sprinkles the remaining cookies on top.
  10. Bake preheated oven until the center is set, around 45 minutes.
  11. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours, or overnight.
  12. Slice it into 12 equal pieces and serve/store leftovers in the fridge.
